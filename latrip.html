    Philippe The Original
    1001 N Alameda St, Los Angeles, CA 90012
    LA institution since 1908. Famous French dip sandwiches, sawdust floors, old-school cafeteria counter. ~15 min drive from the consulate. Note: the dipped bread is wheat — go for the roasted meat, potato salad, hard-boiled eggs, or coleslaw. The vibe alone is worth the stop.

    Hollywood Blvd — Walk of Fame
    Hollywood Blvd & Vine St, Los Angeles, CA 90028
    Head northwest from Chinatown. A slow cruise or short walk along Hollywood Blvd past the Chinese Theatre and Walk of Fame stars. Free, takes 20–30 min, and it's distinctly LA. Then continue north on the 101 toward Griffith Park.

  • ~12:15 pm
    View
    Griffith Observatory
    2800 E Observatory Rd, Los Angeles, CA 90027
    Opens at noon on Tuesdays — perfect timing. Free admission and free parking. The views over the entire LA basin and up to the Hollywood Sign are spectacular. Give yourself 45–60 min. Head up Mount Hollywood Dr to the main parking lot; the lot fills up later in the day so noon arrival is ideal.
